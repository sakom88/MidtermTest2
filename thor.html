Thor

Origin Story of the God of Thunder

Posted by Sarkis Madjarian on November 30, 2022

In the movie Thor, the son of Odin, Thor, is impressionable, impulsive, and confident that he would make a great ruler. After Thor started a war by battling the Frost Giants, his father Odin took away his powers and exiled him to earth, forcing him to show his worthiness of both those things.

Odin's son Thor prepares to ascend to the throne of Asgard, but is interrupted when Frost Giants, secretly allowed in by his brother Loki, attempt to retrieve the Casket. Against Odin's order, Thor travels to Jotunheim to confront Laufey, accompanied by Loki, childhood friend Sif and the Warriors Three: Volstagg, Fandral, and Hogun. A battle ensues until Odin intervenes to save the Asgardians, destroying the fragile truce between the two races.
