Marvel Origin Stories

The way the Marvel movies depict each hero's origin stories is something I find appealing. Each Marvel movie features a hero who faces their unique challenges.

Dr. Strange

The first movie explains how Doctor Strange acquires his abilities following an accident that completely fractured the bones in his hands to the point that he would ended his career as a doctor. He had to struggle and eventually found some wizards in order to be able to use his hands without shaking.

Thor

In the movie Thor, the son of Odin, Thor, is impressionable, impulsive, and confident that he would make a great ruler. After Thor started a war by battling the Frost Giants, his father Odin took away his powers and exiled him to earth, forcing him to show his worthiness of both those things.

Ironman

Billionaire Tony Stark was abducted and forced to create more lethal weapons. Instead, he developed an armored suit, escaped from captivity, and eventually developing a more powerful armor, swearing to defend the world as Iron Man.
